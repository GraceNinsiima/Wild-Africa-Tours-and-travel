Queen Elizabeth National Park

It is a home of many elephants,hippos,congo lions, and other different species of mammals. There are a number of exciting activities like bird watching, tree-climbing lion. It located in western Uganda. Activities include boat ride at Kazinga Channel, game drive in the park, chimpanzee tracking, birding.

Rwenzori Mountains National Park

This is a home of the most beautiful landscapes in the world. Located in the Rwenzori Mountains.

Bwindi Impenetrable National Park

Located in western Uganda.It is filled with over 200 different species of trees, 120 species of mammals. Get a chance to view the mountain gorillas and elephants upclose.

Semiliki National Park

Well known for the Sempaya Hot Springs with water temparature over 100 degrees celicus. The park also has primitive creatures like red-tailed monkeys, elephants, pygmy antelope.

Murchison Falls National Park

Besides the beautiful waterfalls,there are also other creatures like elephants lion, leopard and Cape buffalo.

Kidepo Valley National Park

It houses over different species of birds and mammals. It is located in Kaabong Northern Uganda.
