OUR TRIP PACKAGES

We have a number of trip packages for our clients but you can also design your own trip. All our tours include gorilla tracking at Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.

Our packages include the following

  • 21 days trip
  • 14 days trip
  • 7 days trip
  • 4 days trip

21 DAYS TRIP

Our 21 days trip starts with pick up from Kampala. This trip includes the most important and well known places and national parks in Uganda. We take a tour around the country starting with the source of the Nile in Jinja, Kidepo valley national park, Queen Elizabeth National Park, Semiliki National Park, Queen Elizabeth National Park, Bwindi Impenetrable National Park.
